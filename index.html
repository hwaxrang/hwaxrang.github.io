Research

My research focuses on graph representation learning and knowledge-graph-based models, especially in biomedical and information-centric settings.

I study how large-scale knowledge graphs can be constructed, embedded, and used to improve downstream tasks such as link prediction, recommendation, and knowledge-intensive reasoning under sparse data conditions.

  • Graph representation learning
  • Knowledge graph construction and embedding
  • Biomedical knowledge graphs
  • Knowledge-graph-based reasoning and recommendation

Selected Work

Under Revision · IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI)

Effect of Removing the Prediction Target from a Knowledge Graph on Representation Learning and the Classifier
