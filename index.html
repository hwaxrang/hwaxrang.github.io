Hello.
I'm Hwarang Kwon

I'm an M.S. student in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Myongji University and a member of the DataScience Lab, advised by Professor Jaehee Jung.

My research focuses on graph representation learning and knowledge-graph-based models, especially for biomedical and information-centric applications. I am interested in building knowledge-driven systems that remain effective under sparse-data settings.

Research Interests

Graph Representation Learning Knowledge Graph Embedding Biomedical Knowledge Graphs Drug-Target Interaction Recommendation

I am currently working on cold-start biomedical prediction, continual knowledge graph embedding, and automatic construction of personalized knowledge graphs.
