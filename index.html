About

I am an M.S. student in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Myongji University and a member of the DataScience Lab, advised by Professor Jaehee Jung. I am currently seeking a Ph.D. opportunity where I can further expand my research experience and pursue deeper academic training.

My research centers on Graph Representation Learning and Knowledge Graphs, with particular interests in Knowledge Graph Embedding, Continual Learning, Graph Neural Networks, and a variety of graph-based downstream tasks. I am especially passionate about applying graph-based methodologies to analyze complex data, uncover meaningful insights, and contribute to the advancement of Graph-AI-driven solutions.
