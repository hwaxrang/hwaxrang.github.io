About

My research centers on Graph Representation Learning and Knowledge Graphs, with particular interests in Knowledge Graph Embedding, Graph Neural Networks, AI for Science, and a variety of graph-based downstream tasks. I am especially passionate about applying graph-based methodologies to analyze complex data, uncover meaningful insights, and contribute to the advancement of Graph-AI-driven solutions.
